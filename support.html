Support & Help Center

Everything you need to know about using the BodyTypes app.

Image Encryption

  • Image Encryption: Your photos are encrypted during upload and processed by our private AI engine.

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never share or sell your body data or images to third parties.

  • Delete Anytime: You can delete your account and all associated data by emailing natasha@thesestudios.com.

Onboarding: Weight & Height

We collect this data to calculate your physical scale and proportions relative to standard Kibbe height requirements.

  • Units: You can toggle between LBS/KG or FT-IN/CM using the toggle at the top of the screen.

  • Privacy: We use this data strictly for analysis; it is never shared or sold.

Photo Guidelines

To get an accurate result, follow these visual rules:

Camera Level

The phone should be at waist height, not tilted.

Form-Fitting

Wear clothes that show your silhouette (leggings, swimwear, or gym wear).

Full Body

Ensure your head and feet are both visible in the frame.

Contact & Technical Support

If you encounter a bug or the app freezes during the "Analyse" phase:

  • Restart the app: Most upload issues are resolved by a quick refresh.

  • Email us: If you can't find your answer here, reach out to natasha@thesestudios.com with a screenshot of the issue.
